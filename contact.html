Qinghai

Qinghai, located in northwest China, boasts rich natural landscapes and profound cultural heritage, making it a charming and magical land. Here lies Qinghai Lake, the largest saltwater lake in China, with vast grasslands and majestic Qilian Mountains, forming an unparalleled natural landscape. Qinghai is not only one of the birthplaces of Tibetan culture, but also a paradise for nature enthusiasts and explorers.
