QING HAI

Qinghai, located in northwest China, boasts rich natural landscapes and profound cultural heritage, making it a charming and magical land. Here lies Qinghai Lake, the largest saltwater lake in China, vast grasslands, and the majestic Qilian Mountains, Formed an unparalleled natural painting. Qinghai is not only one of the birthplaces of Tibetan culture, but also a paradise for nature enthusiasts and explorers. Kumbum Monastery, Delingha, Bird Island and other scenic spots here, Take you to experience the rich religious culture and mysterious scenery. The sky in Qinghai is vast, the grasslands are vast, the lakes are clear, and the air is fresh. Whether you long for a natural journey or seek peace of mind, Qinghai can meet all your expectations. Let's walk into this beautiful plateau together and experience the charm and charm of Qinghai.

Food Appreciation

Nianpi: a treasure of noodle dishes in the northwest region

Nianpi is a common traditional snack in Qinghai and northwest regions. Its production process is quite meticulous, requiring first mixing flour into dough, and then repeatedly scrubbing it in clean water, Until gluten and starch are washed out. After precipitation.

Qinghai yogurt: Enjoy a moderate sour and sweet taste

Qinghai yogurt is a highly distinctive dairy product in the Qinghai region. It is renowned for its thick texture, moderate sour and sweet taste, and unique manufacturing process. In the streets and alleys of Qinghai, people can always be seen holding bowls of yogurt, enjoying the delicious food from the plateau.

Hand held lamb: a representative of Qinghai's meat culture

Hand pulled lamb meat is a highly distinctive representative of meat culture in Qinghai region. It uses high-quality lamb meat, with delicious and non greasy flesh, Not pungent or fishy. When eating hand pulled lamb, people usually grab it directly with their hands, dip it in special seasonings.

Miscellaneous Soup: Qinghai's First Choice for Breakfast

Fragmented soup is a highly distinctive breakfast delicacy in Qinghai region. It selects th e internal organs of cattle and sheep, such as heart, liver, lungs, intestines, etc., and carefully cooks them to make a rich soup.
